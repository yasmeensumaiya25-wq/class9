Maths: Polynomials (Chapter 2)
Identity I

Square of Sum

(x + y)² = x² + 2xy + y²
Identity II

Square of Difference

(x - y)² = x² - 2xy + y²
Identity III

Difference of Squares

x² - y² = (x + y)(x - y)
Identity IV

Product of Binomials

(x+a)(x+b) = x²+(a+b)x + ab
Identity V

Square of Trinomial

(x+y+z)² = x²+y²+z² + 2xy+2yz+2zx
Identity VI & VII

Cubes of Binomials

(x+y)³ = x³+y³+3xy(x+y)
(x-y)³ = x³-y³-3xy(x-y)
Identity VIII

Special Triple Identity

x³+y³+z³-3xyz =
(x+y+z)(x²+y²+z²-xy-yz-zx)

If x+y+z = 0, then x³+y³+z³ = 3xyz.

Maths: Surface Areas & Volumes
Cuboid

Area & Volume

TSA = 2(lb+bh+hl)
Vol = l × b × h

Lateral Surface Area = 2h(l+b).

Cylinder

Area & Volume

CSA = 2πrh
Vol = πr²h

TSA = 2πr(r+h).

Cone

Area & Volume

CSA = πrl
Vol = 1/3 πr²h

l = √(r²+h²).

Sphere

Area & Volume

Surface Area = 4πr²
Vol = 4/3 πr³

Motion

Equations of Motion

Force

Laws of Force

F = m × a

Gravitation

Gravity Laws

g = GM/R²
Work & Energy

Energy Forms

Sound

Wave Characteristics
